Video Production and Video Editing


Since I was younger I always enjoyed making short, fun movies with my friends. Over the years, I began making videos documenting all of the trips and adventures I go on utilizing drones and action cameras. I love being able to capture new and unique perspectives while making these videos, and just like my photography, I like telling a story through these videos I produce. Besides many years of personal experience, I also have taken over five years of video production and editing classes throughout my education. Currently, I am working on my major Digital Media at the Santa Rosa Junior College. Video production and editing has always been a big part of my life, and I want to be able to apply my skills to any projects you might have.
