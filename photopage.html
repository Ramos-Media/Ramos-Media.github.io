Photography and Photo Editing


I've always been fascinated by taking pictures on my smartphone, but a few years ago I received my first DSLR camera - the Canon Rebel T7. I immediately fell in love with using it for photography, and upgraded my gear selection by purchasing several high-quality lenses and accessories. My particular interest in photography is being able to capture meaningful moments through the lens and share it with others. I am inspired by the infinite possibilities and creativity that photography offers, and how it can help us see more of the world. I make images of things I like to do, places I go, and subjects that are interesting to me.


A great photograph is one that fully expresses what one feels, in the deepest sense, about what is being photographed.
- Ansel Adams

Photo Editing

Using professional-grade software like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop, I can bring out the best in your photos. Using techniques like analyzing the image histogram and assessing the composition, I can provide these simple and advanced edits:

  • Crop to best composition
  • Create more natural or vibrant colors
  • Make the image pop more
  • Add filters to change image appearance
